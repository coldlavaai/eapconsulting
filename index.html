About Earlam & Partners

Earlam & Partners is a trusted consultancy firm dedicated to supporting the cotton industry with data-driven insights and expert guidance. Our team combines decades of experience with innovative approaches to market analysis, offering clients the tools they need to hedge risks and maximise profitability.

We provide bi-weekly market reports and bespoke consultancy services, ensuring our clients stay ahead in an ever-changing market.

Our Reports

We offer a range of market reports designed to provide comprehensive insights into key trends and opportunities:

  • Thursday Report – A detailed mid-week market update.
  • Weekend Report – A comprehensive summary of the week’s market activity.
  • Soybean Report – Insights into the soybean market and its impact on cotton.
  • Macros Report – Analysis of macroeconomic factors influencing the industry.
  • Technical Analysis Report – In-depth technical insights for informed decision-making.
